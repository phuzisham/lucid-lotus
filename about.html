BODI SAHTVA

Here's a taste of my story:

My name is Cassidy, otherwise known as the human foam roller! I am a traveling integrative and functional massage therapist currently making a home right here in Portland, OR. It would be easy to tell you that my journey to the city of roses was intentional, but it was through a series of synchronicities coupled with my innate desire to always lead with intuition.

Throughout my formative years and well into my academic career, many of my natural gifts flourished in a holistic caregiver role, eventually causing me to shift focus towards Massage Therapy.

After successfully finishing top in my class from Laâ€™ James International College in 2009, I also made the decision to move forward with my newfound love of human anatomy by pursuing Esthetics as well. Upon completion of the aforementioned program, I moved to Colorado where I was able to truly craft, master and share my art.

Welcomed into a thriving music scene, I was able to combine my love for music with my passion for holistic healing by working at major music venues to provide treatments for touring musicians prior to their show performances. Over the last decade I've been able to collaborate and connect with strangers around the world, turning those opportunities into tangible ways to contribute to those around me. I've now found myself in Oregon, where I am building the foundation of my dreams. This life experience has continued to be so beautiful even through the good and bad, allowing me to become a stronger human that leads with love, appreciation and a commitment to self-preservation and healing. Everyday I believe we must continue to unlearn the conditioning of our upbringing in order to be the best version of ourselves. In my travels of finding myself, I've been able to heal through the ability to heal others. The human experience is a party and I am here for it.
