BODI SAHTVA

Heres a taste of my story: Here's a taste of my story: Curious Candor Cassidy, the human foam roller. Being born and raised on the Mississippi River in a small town in Iowa has allowed me to flourish. Throughout my youth, I spent most of my time gardening, foraging mushrooms hunting and mastering the art of elusive shenanigans.

In early adulthood, I spent my first two years as an adult at Iowa State University, majoring in Animal Ecology, striving to become a veterinarian. During one of my internships, I had an epiphany. Witnessing people suffer sparked the deep rooted empathy inside within myself. I have always had the gift of a connector and now wanted to help people feel their best. This is when I realized that I wanted to work with humans. I decided to switch to Massage Therapy in 2009, where I finished top in of my class.

I had fallen so deep in love with human anatomy that I decided to pursue Esthetics as well. Upon completion of the program, I moved to Colorado. Thriving in Colorado allowed me to really craft, master, and share my art. Over the last decade, most of my time has been spent networking, collaborating, learning, witnessing, sharing, healing and surviving this human experience. Montana drew me in a couple times, where I found community and myself more than ever. I soon followed my heart to Oregon. Here, I am building the foundation of my dreams. It’s my mission to learn how to help create balance in all aspects of my life. My relentless passion lies in holistic healing.

What a beautiful journey this life has been, using take my experiences-good and bad, to be a stronger human. I choose to lead with love. In my travels of finding myself, I’ve been able to heal myself through the ability to heal others. The human experience is a party and I’m here for it.
