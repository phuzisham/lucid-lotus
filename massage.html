What you can expect from Lucid Lotus Biowellness

As a massage therapist, my goal is to utilize every human sense to provide the best full body experience possible. My detail oriented approach allows me to fully immerse my client into an environment of healing by using calming music, relaxing soundscapes, soothing aromatherapy and rare oil techniques. I have an extensive history of working at a variety of spas, chiropractic and acupuncture offices and other holistic health spaces that have given me a unique blend of modalities to share. I specialize in deep tissue, myofascial release, cupping and trigger point therapy. My intentional approach is slow, regenerative and communicative with each and every breath.

Oils

Typically, the natural oils I use include jojoba, coconut and arnica, with others available upon request. I also have a wide variety of essential oils to add to any service, which include some of my favorites from Savitur Botanicals. These oils are sourced from biodynamically certified growers around the world to support a holistic, ecological and ethical approach to farming on a global scale.
